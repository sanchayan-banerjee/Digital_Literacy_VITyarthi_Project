# Task 1: Digital Literacy Presentation - Description

This presentation is created to highlight the importance of digital literacy for students in the modern world. The slide is designed in a 1920 x 1080 format and covers key topics such as email etiquette, essential digital tools and professional online presence. 

The email etiquette section explains how to write clear subject lines, maintain a professional tone and proofread messages before sending. The digital tools section introduces commonly used platforms like productivity suites, learning management systems and research tools that support academic work. The professional online presence section focuses on building a strong digital identity through consistent branding and meaningful content. 

The design uses a structured layout with clear headings, visuals and icons to make the information easy to understand. Overall, the presentation aims to educate students on using digital technologies responsibly and effectively for academic and professional growth.
