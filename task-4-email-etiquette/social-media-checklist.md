# Do's and Don'ts Checklist for Responsible Social Media Use as a College Student:
## List of Do's to be practised are as follows:-
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments.
- Think before posting and ensure your content reflects positively on  you.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting security settings and limiting personal information.
- Engage positively with others and contribute to meaningful discussions.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

## List of Don'ts to be aware of are as follows:-
- Do not share offensive, abusive or inappropriate content.
- Avoid posting confidential or sensitive personal information. 
- Do not engage in online argments or toxic discussions.
- Avoid spreading rumors or unverified news.
- Do not post content that could potentially harm your academic or professional reputation.
