# Task 2: Portfolio Reflection

## Overview
In this task, I created and updated my profiles on platforms such as GitHub , LinkedIn and Kaggle. The objective was to build a professional digital presence and understsand how online profiles represent an individual.

## What I Did
- Created a GitHub profile README with my academic details and goals.
- Updated my LinkedIn profile by adding my education details.
- Took screenshots of my profiles as proof of completion.

## What I Learned
Through this task, I learned the importance of maintaining a professional online identity. I understood how platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle can be used to showcase my skills, projects and academic background effectively.

## Challenges Faced
One challenge I faced was capturing screenshots that clearly showed both my profile identity and required details like the education section. Organizing the files properly in the required folder structure was also slightly challenging.

## Conclusion
Overall, this task helped me improve my understanding of digital portfolios and professional networking. It also gave me practical experience in organizing and presenting digital information clearly.
