# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist:

## General Cyber Safety Tips:
- **Use strong and unique passwords :** Create passwords using a mix of uppercase,lowercase, numbers and symbols. Avoid reusing passwords across platforms.
- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) :** Turn on 2FA for email, banking apps and social media accounts to add an extra layer of protection.
- **Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities :** Do not access banking or payment apps on public Wi-Fi networks like those in cafes or railway stations.
- **Be cautious of phishing links and messages :** Do not click on suspicious links received through SMS, email or WhatsApp, even if they appear to be from trusted sources.
- **Download apps only from trusted sources :** Install apps only form official app stores like Google Play Store. Avoid downloading APK files from unknown websites.
- **Keep your devices and apps updated :** Regular updates fix security vulnerabilities and protect your device from malware. 

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
- **Never approve unknown UPI collect requests :** Always verify the sender before approving any payment request. Receiving money does not require entering a UPI PIN.
- **Do not share OTP, UPI PIN or banking details :** Banks or officials never ask for sensitive details. Sharing them can result in financial loss.
- **Verify QR codes before scanning :** Fraudsters may send fake QR codes claiming cashback or refunds. Scanning them can lead to money being debited.

## Personal and Social Media Safety:
- **Limit sharing personal information online :** Avoid posting sensitive details like phone number, address or college ID publicly on social media.
- **Always log out from shared devices :** Ensure you log out of your accounts when using public or shared computers.

## Reporting Cyber Crimes:
If you become a victim of cyber fraud, report immediately:
- **National Cyber Crime Portal :** https://cybercrime.gov.in
- **Helpline Number :** 1930

Timely reporting can help in recovering lost funds and preventing further damage.
